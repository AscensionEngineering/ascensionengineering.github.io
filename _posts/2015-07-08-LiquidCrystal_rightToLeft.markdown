	LiquidCrystal::rightToLeft


	void LiquidCrystal::rightToLeft();

This sets the LCD to displat text right to left (think Hebrew).

Usage:

	LiquidCrystal lcd(8, 9, 4, 5, 6, 7);
	lcd.begin(16, 2);
	lcd.rightToLeft();
